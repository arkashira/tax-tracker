# 🚀 tax-tracker
**Power individuals and businesses with simplified tax management.** Comprehensive tax management tool that simplifies tracking of expenses and income, with automated tax estimation features to help users avoid underpayment penalties.

## Why tax-tracker?
* **User-friendly interface**: Designed for non-accountants, reducing the learning curve for tax management.
* **Automated tax estimation**: Helps users avoid underpayment penalties with accurate and timely estimates.
* **Comprehensive expense tracking**: Simplifies the process of tracking expenses and income, making tax season less stressful.
* **Customizable**: Allows users to tailor the tool to their specific needs and preferences.
* **Secure**: Prioritizes user data security and privacy, ensuring sensitive information is protected.
* **Regular updates**: Receives regular updates and maintenance to ensure compliance with changing tax laws and regulations.
* **Community-driven**: Encourages community involvement and feedback to continuously improve the tool.
