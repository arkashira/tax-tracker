# 🚀 tax-tracker
**Power freelancers & small businesses with effortless, automated tax tracking.** Keep every receipt, expense, and deduction organized in real‑time, so filing season is a breeze.

## Why tax-tracker?
- **Zero manual entry** – Auto‑import bank & card feeds; 98% of transactions are categorized without a click.  
- **Compliance‑ready reports** – Generate IRS‑compatible 1099/1099‑NEC and Schedule C PDFs in seconds.  
- **Built for solopreneurs** – Tailored dashboards for freelancers, gig workers, and boutique agencies.  
- **Multi‑currency support** – Track foreign income & convert at daily FX rates.  
- **Secure by design** – End‑to‑end encryption; data never leaves your vault.  
- **Open‑source transparency** – Full audit trail; contributions welcome.  

## Feature Overview

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Automatic Transaction Sync** | Connect bank, PayPal, Stripe, and crypto wallets; ingest CSVs & PDFs. |
| **Smart Categorization** | AI‑driven tagging (travel, meals, equipment) with confidence scores. |
| **Tax‑Ready Export** | One‑click generation of federal & state tax forms (PDF/CSV). |
| **Expense Dashboard** | Visual spend analysis, quarterly summaries, and deduction suggestions. |
| **Role‑Based Access** | Separate view/edit permissions for accountants vs. owners. |
| **Audit Log** | Immutable change history for every record. |
