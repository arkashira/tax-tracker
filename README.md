# 🚀 tax-tracker
**Power individuals and businesses with simplified tax management and automated estimation.** Comprehensive tax management tool that simplifies tracking of expenses and income.

## Why tax-tracker?
- **User-friendly**: Designed for non-accountants, reducing the learning curve for tax management.
- **Automated estimation**: Helps users avoid underpayment penalties with accurate tax estimation features.
- **Comprehensive tracking**: Simplifies the tracking of expenses and income for individuals and businesses.
- **Customizable**: Allows users to tailor the tool to their specific needs and preferences.
- **Secure**: Prioritizes data security and privacy to protect sensitive user information.
- **Scalable**: Designed to grow with the user's needs, whether individual or business.

## Feature Overview
| Feature | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Expense tracking | Easily log and categorize expenses for accurate tax reporting |
| Income tracking | Simplified income tracking for individuals and businesses |
| Automated tax estimation | Reduce the risk of underpayment penalties with accurate estimates |
| User-friendly interface | Intuitive design for non-accountants and accountants alike |
| Customizable | Tailor the tool to your specific needs and preferences |
